FAQ

Common Questions

Is it really free?
Yes, 100% free. We use Groq's free API tier which gives you 30 requests per minute ‚Äî more than enough for any interview. No credit card needed.
Do I need to install anything?
No. It's a web app that runs in your phone browser. No downloads, no installations. You can add it to your home screen for app-like experience.
Can the interviewer detect it?
No. Nothing runs on your interview computer. Your phone is a completely separate device that screen sharing, task managers, and proctoring software cannot access.
What if they ask me to share my screen?
No problem! Your computer is completely clean. Share your entire screen, open task manager, alt-tab freely ‚Äî there's nothing to find. All answers appear only on your phone.
How fast are the answers?
Typically 2-3 seconds from when you stop recording to when the answer appears. Fast enough to glance at key points before responding naturally.
Is my data safe?
All your data (resume, API key, settings) is stored locally on your phone using browser storage. Nothing is sent to our servers. Audio is processed through Groq's encrypted API and not stored.
Which phones work?
Any smartphone with a modern browser ‚Äî iPhone (Safari), Android (Chrome), or any tablet. If it can open a website, it works.
Can I practice before my interview?
Yes! Practice mode has company-specific questions for Google, Amazon, Meta, Microsoft, Apple, and startups. AI evaluates your answers with scores and improvement tips.
